About Me

Get to Know Me a Little Better.

Hey, I'm Elizabeth but my friends call me Lizzy! I've been taking photos for almost three years now and have loved every minute. I find photos truthful and for that reason, they are my favorite form of art.

With my photos, I try to tell a story. I want them to not just show what a moment looked like but how it felt. I want them to capture what our memories cannot.

Beyond photography, I am attending Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. Although Utah is where I currently live, I still claim Oregon as my home. I'm not sure where I'll be or what i'll be doing for the next year but I know it's going to be good!

Probably the most important thing to know about me is my testimony of the atonement of Jesus Christ and in our Heavenly Father. After all, It is because of our Lord and Savior that I have such pretty places and beautiful subjects to photograph :)
